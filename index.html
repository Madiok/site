Japanese dream

Nissan Skyline - a car manufactured in Japan since 1957, first by the firm Prince Motor, and then by Nissan Motor, who bought Prince in 1966. By now, 13 generations of this car have been produced.

Prince Skyline

First generation

The first Skyline with the factory designation ALSI-1 descended from the conveyor belt from 1957 to 1963 under the brand name Prince and by the standards of Japan was considered a luxury car. A total of 33,759 items were sold, mostly with sedan and wagon bodies.
